Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data
<

Effective Date: April 16, 2025

1. What Information We Collect

When you use our website or services, we may collect the following types of data:

  • Username, Discord ID, Minecraft IGN
  • Email address (if provided voluntarily)
  • Transaction metadata (no payment credentials)
  • IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Activity logs and interactions on our site

2. Why We Collect Data

We collect this data to:

  • Fulfill your purchases and deliver digital goods
  • Detect fraud or abuse on our platform
  • Provide user support and verify identities
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Analyze server performance and site usage

3. Who We Share It With

Your data is not sold. It may be shared only with:

  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe, Tebex)
  • Hosting and database providers
  • Law enforcement upon valid request

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to remember user preferences and monitor site performance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work correctly.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for operations, customer support, and legal compliance. You may request deletion of your information unless otherwise required by law.

6. Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Withdraw consent or object to processing
  • Request deletion of your personal data

7. Protection and Security

We use encryption, access controls, and other technologies to secure your data. Despite our efforts, no platform is 100% secure.

8. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from users under 13 years old. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review it regularly. Continued use of our services indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, reach out via our Discord or support email available on the website.
